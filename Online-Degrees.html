Study entirely Online

Get a Foreign Degree without leaving your Home Country

Many universities around the world award their degrees entirely online which you can pursue from the comfort of your home or office without having to leave your home country. Since these undergraduate and postgraduate degrees follow the exact same curriculum like their offline counterparts taught on campus, they carry the identical weight and prestige you would expect. If an online degree from a foreign university, not mentioned here, interests you, please let us know and we will take care of your admissions process from end to end.
We facilitate these degrees through the following model which we will customize for you to meet your precise academic and career goals:

  • Identify the right country to suit your geographical preferences
  • Identify the right online degree through a mentoring and advising interaction with you
  • Admit you to the degree of your choice

Since we possess extensive experience in the study abroad and overseas education space, we will recommend the right country, degree and concentration for you based on multiple online interactions with you. You can also attend these universities as an international student and pursue the entire degree on campus with our guidance.

Benefits of pursuing an International Online degree from your Home Country

  • Significantly higher level and quality of guidance, education and training when compared with some universities in your home country
  • A unique combination of instructor-led and Self-paced study
  • Massive online resources, research libraries and repositories to which you will have unlimited access
  • Keep your full-time job and earn an online degree simultaneously
  • Degree fully recognized in your home country
  • Continue your studies without interruption while traveling
  • Global recognition of your degree
  • 100% recognized and accredited degrees
  • Flexible schedules
  • No boarding and lodging expenses
  • No visa required
  • Possibility of your completing the final year of your degree on campus, thus qualifying you for a work permit in your destination country

Down Side

While online degrees from foreign universities involve the same achievement levels and recognition as offline, on-campus taught degrees, they usually cost almost the same as offline degrees. We will make every effort to procure a scholarship for you to reduce your tuition costs. Online degrees from universities abroad also do not come with any type of work permits in your destination country unless you qualify for a final year top up attendance program.
