How It Works

Jumpstart your College Journey

Your journey commences soon after you register with us based on information we will provide you. The first step is to learn more about you and understand the career direction you plan to follow. One of our team members, who is a certified career counsellor, will conduct a counselling session with you if you are unclear about which academic pathway to pursue. And there is more!

Interaction and Communication

We will communicate with you post registration using a variety of platforms such as email, WhatsApp, phone and Zoom. Call our office during regular working hours including Sundays and access our services without any interruption. The college admissions journey is a complex one with tons of whistle stops. We will help you navigate the entire journey.
