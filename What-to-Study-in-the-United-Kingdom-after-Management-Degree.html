What to Study in the United Kingdom after a Management Degree

  • What to Study in the United Kingdom after a Management Degree

Just completed a B.Com. Or a BBA degree in your home country? You can now add significant value to your undergraduate degree by studying in the UK. There is even better news!

Why Study in the UK?

The common notion that education in the UK is expensive is merely a myth. Annual tuition can be as low as GBP 7,000 which will enable you to earn a bachelor’s or master’s degree from an accredited and recognized university in the UK. These figures are approximate and will depend greatly on the university, the degree program, and its location. There are scholarships for which we will apply on your behalf once you enroll with us. This will reduce your tuition costs even further.

Exceptionally high quality of education, outstanding placement opportunities, global exposure, and a seamless visa process for international students are some of the other reasons why you should seriously consider the UK for your management degree or any other degree for that matter.

What to Study

Fields you can pursue while studying for a degree in the UK include:

  • Accounting
  • Actuarial Science
  • Banking
  • Finance
  • Insurance
  • International Banking
  • International Business
  • International Finance
  • Investment
  • Strategy and Consulting
  • Human Resource Management
  • Marketing
  • Sales
  • AI and Business
  • Sports Management
  • Entertainment Management
  • Business Analytics
  • Many more!

We have developed a bucket list of 44 management concentrations, which is available to you for the asking. Please ask for this PDF by writing to us.

Working in the UK

If you pursue either a bachelor’s or master’s degree in finance, accounting, insurance, taxation, or any other business-related subject, you will be eligible for the two-year Graduate Route work visa.
This visa status can be supplemented with the five-year Skilled Workers Visa. Ask us about these visas during a Zoom session, and we will brief you in comprehensive detail.

The entire counselling team at Alphabet Education is standing by to extend a helping hand as you plan your upward-bound journey to the UK. We provide end-to-end admission support, including financial documentation support and visa filing support, regardless of which country happens to be your home country.
