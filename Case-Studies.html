Case Studies

Alphabet Education has many sagas to narrate and many success stories to share showcasing our students whose journeys have taken them to unprecedented heights.

"A chartered accountant from Delhi by profession working in corporate investments, Devashish possesses the unique accolade of having pursued two master’s degrees at Cornell back-to-back. Since he was focused entirely on generating investment capital for the hospitality industry in India, Devashish wanted to pursue a master’s degree in hotel management at Cornell. Additionally, he realized, and rightly so, that an MBA from the Johnson School of Business at Cornell would eventually play a pivotal role in elevating his career. He pursued both degrees and is today managing a leading global organization."

Devashish Sharma

Cornell University

"A graduate of the Indian Institute of Technology – Kanpur, Mehar always wanted to be a robotics engineer. However, her undergraduate degree was in a different field. We guided her throughout her degree and encouraged her to participate in robotics competitions, conduct supervised research in robotics, and even build solutions for the industry. An incredibly hard- working and dedicated student, Mehar juggled multiple balls including academics, extra- curriculars and robotics to earn an admit to the robotics department at Carnegie Mellon University."

Mehar Goenka

Carnegie Mellon University

"Ishan dreamt day and night about flying in space right through his formative years. Spaceships, rockets and space stations fascinated him to the core. When he got in touch with the admissions team at Alphabet Education, we recommended that he further solidify his profile by learning as much as possible regarding advances in space science. Ishan gained an admit to Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University, the go-to destination for those dreamers who wish to explore the infinite boundaries of space."

Ishan Hudda

Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University

"Nandini is a mathematical child prodigy starting as early as nursery school. After pursuing a bachelor’s degree in mathematics from St. Stephen’s College, University of Delhi, where she bewildered her faculty with her mathematical prowess, Nandini gained an admit to the University of Oxford where she earned a master’s degree in computational mathematics."

Nandini Bhatia

University of Oxford

"While working at Goldman Sachs as an analyst, Manasve soon realized that given the incredible pace at which technology was progressing, she would soon need to upscale. Instead of taking random courses from here, there and beyond, Manasve decided to pursue a master’s degree in computer science. Our team navigated her mission eventually leading her to the University of Southern California from where she earned a master’s degree in computer science."

Manasve Dahiya

University of Southern California
