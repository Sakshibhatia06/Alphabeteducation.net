Message from Our Founder

Dear Applicant,
It is with immense pride and pleasure that I welcome you to our company and its entire team. When I arrived in India in 2001 after having taught at three US universities, the University of Alabama, University of Cincinnati and Blue Ash University, I realized that many students who had just completed their 12th grade were absolutely clueless about which career trajectory to embrace, which career path to pursue, and which universities to consider for post secondary study. Alphabet Education was born amidst this confluence of confusion and clarity.

It is our collective endeavor to leave no stone unturned to help you achieve success with your dream career. Engineering, law, medicine, media, communication, we are game! My team and I will treat you not as a statistic but as an individual. Since our services are fully online, it makes no difference where you live. Thanks to modern technology, we can now communicate seamlessly across geographies without paying any attention to physical boundaries.

We are constantly standing by to answer your questions, make suggestions and help you navigate the complex admissions journey. Facilitating scholarships for you is our top priority given the high cost of college education in India. If there is a specific niche service that you require, we will surely attempt to deliver it to you since we customize every applicant's case. I can always be reached directly on phone, email and Whatsapp seven days a week including national and public holidays. Do get in touch with me and I will help you transition to the next level.

Sincerely,

— Rajesh

Alphabet Education
Noida
Phone and Whatsapp: +91 9810253145

PS: We welcome inquiries from those applicants who plan to apply for admission to bachelor's, master's, MBA, MPhil and Ph.D. degrees across India.
