Message from Our Founder

Dear Applicant,
It is with immense pride and pleasure that I welcome you to our company and its entire team. When I arrived in India in 2001 after having taught at three US universities, the University of Alabama, University of Cincinnati, and Blue Ash University, I realized that many students who had just completed their 12th grade in India were absolutely clueless about which career trajectory to embrace, which career path to pursue, and which universities to consider for postsecondary study abroad. The same was true about students planning to pursue postgraduate degrees. Confusion galore!

Alphabet Education was born amidst this confluence of confusion and clarity.
It is our collective endeavor as a team to leave no stone unturned to help you achieve success with your dream career. Engineering, law, management, medicine, media, communication, we are game! My team and I will treat you not as a statistic but as an individual. Since our services are fully online, it makes no difference where you live in the world. Thanks to modern technology, we can now communicate seamlessly across geographies without paying any attention to physical boundaries and time zones.

We are constantly standing by to answer your questions, make suggestions, and help you navigate the complex admissions journey. Facilitating scholarships for you is our top priority, given the high cost of college education worldwide.

If there is a specific niche service related to college admissions that you require, we will surely attempt to deliver it to you since we customize every applicant's case. I can always be reached directly by phone, email, and WhatsApp seven days a week. My team is available 24X7 through email, Zoom, and WhatsApp. Do get in touch with me, and I will help you transition to your next milestone of academic and professional success.

Sincerely,

— Rajesh

Alphabet Education
Noida
Phone and WhatsApp: +91 7678667629

PS: We welcome inquiries from those applicants who plan to apply for admission to bachelor's, master's, MBA, MPhil, and Ph.D. degrees across continents.

About the Founder

Rajesh earned his bachelor’s degree in English with honors from St. Stephen’s College, University of Delhi, India, and a Master’s degree in English from the University of Cincinnati, USA. He possesses many years of rich and highly focused experience in the college admissions space.

Rajesh’s students have gained admission to top universities such as Cornell, University of Chicago, Duke University, New York University, University of Southern California, UC Berkeley, UCLA, UC San Diego, Carnegie Mellon University, University of Toronto, University of British Columbia, University of Oxford, King’s College of London, University College London, London School of Economics and Political Science, University of Saint Andrews, University of Edinburgh, University of Melbourne and many more. References are welcome once you register with Alphabet Education.

Applicants whom Rajesh and his team have mentored have gone on to exciting careers in fields within the professional sphere including AI, robotics, IT, international relations, engineering, strategy and consulting, business analytics, entrepreneurship, journalism, sustainability, finance, marketing, supply chain, human resources, healthcare management, decision sciences, retail management, engineering management, life sciences, law, medicine, architecture, social work, luxury and fashion management, real estate management and much more. He works closely with college aspirants to help them pursue exciting career opportunities worldwide. Many applicants whom he has mentored have joined top MNCs like Google, Amazon, Microsoft, Uber, Facebook, Tesla, IBM, Under Armor, PWC, McKinsey, Boston Consulting Group, Bain, Deloitte Consulting, Booz & Company, Chartis Group, Brattle Group, P&G, GE, CNN, and PepsiCo.
