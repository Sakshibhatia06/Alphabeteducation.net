Study in the United States

Studying in the United States remains a critical decision which offers a unique blend of world- class academic rigor, and unparalleled career growth. Alphabet Education can get you there! We will select the right universities for you based on your career preference and domain inclination through a an in-depth brainstorming session, and pilot your applications for you from start to finish.

Why the USA?

As home to many of the world's highly ranked universities, the U.S. continues to set global standards for higher education both in areas of knowledge as well as research.

Global Academic Leadership

The U.S. consistently dominates international university rankings. In the latest QS World University Rankings, for instance, American universities hold five of the top 10 spots, with the Massachusetts Institute of Technology maintaining its decade-long position at #1. Universities like New York University, Duke, and the University of California Berkeley are globally recognized for high academic standards and rigorous research outcomes. Alphabet Education is honored to have sent super achievers to many of these universities including Ivy League, Public Ivy and Private Ivy schools. You can study there too!

Unmatched Flexibility

A hallmark of the American pedagogical system is its academic freedom. Students often have the freedom and flexibility to take diverse courses and subjects for two years before optionally declaring a major. You can still declare your major at the time of your application to undergraduate study.

Customized Learning

You can blend disciplines, such as majoring in computer science with a concentration in fine arts, thus creating a highly personalized academic pathway. You can also pursue a double major at all universities in the United states. Almost all universities offer study abroad programs where you will experience global education, diversity, and culture at its best.

Strategic Career Launchpad

Studying in the U.S. is a powerful investment in long-term upward career mobility. International graduates on F-1 student visas can work in the U.S. for up to 12 months after graduation. Students in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) fields may apply for an additional 24-month extension, totaling 36 months of work eligibility. The H-1b visa is the next step and offers six years of full-time employment authorization, sufficient time for you to file your Green Card application. Alphabet Education’s legal team will navigate the entire process for you as one of your several program options. Many campuses are strategically located near innovation hubs such as the Silicon Valley, Research Triangle, New York City, or Boston, which facilitates direct networking with global giants like Google, Meta, Citibank and Microsoft.

Innovation and Research

U.S. universities are at the forefront of cutting-edge technologies. They invest billions in research and provide students with access to state-of-the-art laboratories and the opportunity to work alongside Nobel Laureates and industry pioneers. This "learning by doing" approach ensures that students are not merely studying theory but actively shaping the future of their chosen disciplines for posterity.

Vibrant Global Communities

With over 1.2 million international students hosted currently, U.S. campuses are cultural "melting pots". Students from over 190 countries create a rich, inclusive, and diverse environment where you can celebrate holidays like Holi or Lunar New Year alongside your peers. Beyond the classroom, thousands of student-run clubs, sports teams, and professional student groups support personal growth and lifelong international connections. While the journey involves careful planning regarding visas and costs, the high Return on Investment characterized by high starting salaries and global degree recognition makes the U.S. a top choice for super- achieving students like you. Alphabet Education alumni, currently employed in the United States, are drawing seven figure salaries touching $300K and higher annually.

Concluding Thoughts

Please disregard the negative publicity the United States has garnered recently. As long as you obey the laws of the land and maintain your visa status, focus on academics instead of agitation, and maintain a stellar academic record at your dream university in the United States, You will constantly remain not only in the safe zone but also in the success zone. So why not schedule a free online session with us and help us put your apprehensions to rest? Call today!
