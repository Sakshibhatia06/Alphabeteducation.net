The Various Categories of Personally Identifiable Data

Definition

"Personally Identifiable Data" refers to those types of information that can be used to either identify or can be leveraged to place, identify, contact, communicate with or locate the person or persons to whom the information pertains. This includes but is not limited to:

  • Your Name and any pen names or aliases if applicable
  • Your physical postal address or your physical mailing address
  • Your primary phone number
  • Your alternate phone number
  • Your email address or addresses
  • Your government-issued identification number such as a social security or TIN number
  • Your fax number if applicable
  • Your personal and confidential financial information such as bank information required primarily to address the admissions requirements of your target universities in India
  • Your Facebook, X, LinkedIn, Pinterest, Instagram or login and password information if you choose to exercise the option of using your social media platforms to log into our website when such a facility becomes available on AlphabetEducation.net
  • Any other information that can help us authenticate your identity

"Personally identifiable data" does not include information that has been gathered anonymously. It also does not include standard demographic or geo information that has been gathered from website visitors or customers without authenticating their identity.
